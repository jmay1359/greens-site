How It Works

GreenOS emulates the full lifecycle of a controlled environment agriculture system using real-world data inputs from Excel. The process includes:

  • Input Configuration: Upload Excel files
  • Simulation: Model crop growth, costs, and revenue
  • Results: Generate reports in HTML, Excel, and console

Key Requirements and Features

Cost Modeling

Track cultivation, utilities, labor, packaging, and transport costs per crop per period.

Crop Simulation

Generate per-period output by crop and by market segment (retail/foodservice).

Revenue Forecasting

Link output to market pricing schedules for revenue projections.

Environment Emulation

Simulate climate variables like CO₂, temperature, and humidity.

Reporting Engine

Export professional-grade reports in multiple formats with rich visual support.
